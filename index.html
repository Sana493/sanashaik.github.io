About Me

Turning data into
meaningful stories.

I'm a Data Science student at UNC Charlotte with a passion for understanding how data shapes real-world outcomes — especially in health, behavior, and social systems.

I enjoy building clean analyses, thoughtful visualizations, and machine learning models that reveal patterns worth caring about. My work spans healthcare prediction, mental health signals, sleep science, and ethical data practice.

Outside of code, I think deeply about how the tools we build affect people — and how responsibility should be baked into every model we deploy.
